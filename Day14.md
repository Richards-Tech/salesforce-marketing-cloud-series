## 📊 Data Views in Salesforce Marketing Cloud

---

### 🔹 What are Data Views?
Data Views are system-generated tracking tables in SFMC that store data about:  
👉 Emails Sent  
👉 Opens  
👉 Clicks  
👉 Bounces  
👉 Unsubscribes  

💡 **Simple Understanding:**  
Data Views = “Backend tracking system of your email activities”  

---

### 🧠 Why Data Views are Important?
✔ Track campaign performance  
✔ Build audience segmentation  
✔ Identify engaged vs inactive users  
✔ Improve targeting strategy  

---

### 🔧 Most Used Data Views in Projects
- 👉 **_Sent** → Tracks all email sends  
- 👉 **_Open** → Tracks email opens  

💡 Perfect for retargeting campaigns

### 🚀 Pro Tip
Combine: _Sent + _Open + _Click

###  To create segments like:
✔ Highly Engaged  
✔ Partially Engaged  
✔ Inactive Users  

### 💡 Key Takeaway
Data Views = Tracking + Segmentation + Insights  
👉 Mastering them helps you debug campaigns, build smarter segments, and improve targeting strategies.
