# 📅 Day 5 – SFMC Learning Series | Learn with Me

## 📧 Email Studio in SFMC

In Salesforce Marketing Cloud:  
👉 Many people can design emails  
👉 But don’t understand how Email Studio works with data, sending logic, and tracking  

So let’s understand this step by step 👇

---

### 🔹 What is Email Studio?
Email Studio is used to:  
- Create and design emails  
- Manage subscribers and data  
- Send emails across campaigns  
- Track performance  

👉 It is the core communication tool in SFMC  

---

### 🔹 1. Email Creation (Content Builder Integration)
Emails are created using:  
- Drag & Drop blocks  
- HTML emails  
- Personalization strings  

📌 **Keep in Mind:**  
If the fields are missing in DE → email fails  

---

### 🔹 2. Subscriber & Data Handling
Email Studio works with:  
- Sendable Data Extensions  
- All Subscribers List  

👉 Important fields:  
- Subscriber Key  
- Email Address  

📌 **Keep in Mind:**  
Wrong mapping =  
❌ Email not sent  
❌ Duplicate users  

---

### 🔹 3. Send Methods (How Email is Triggered)
Emails can be sent using:  
- 👉 User-Initiated Send (Manual)  
- 👉 Journey Builder (Automated flow)  
- 👉 Automation Studio (Scheduled send)  

📌 **Example:**  
User books ticket → Journey triggers → Confirmation email sent  

---

### 🔹 4. Send Classification
Defines:  
- Sender Name  
- From Email  
- Unsubscribe logic  

👉 Ensures:  
- Compliance  
- Proper email delivery  

📌 **Keep in Mind:**  
Wrong configuration → email blocked or fails  

---

### 🔹 5. Personalization & Dynamic Content
Used to send customized emails  

👉 **Example:**  
- Different offers for Gold vs Silver users  
- Personalized greeting  

---

### 🔹 6. Tracking & Reporting
Email Studio provides:  
- Open Rate  
- Click Rate  
- Bounce Rate  
- Unsubscribe  

👉 Helps measure campaign performance  

---

### 🔹 Real End-to-End Flow
Contact enters Data Extension → Journey Builder triggers → Email Studio sends email → User opens/clicks → Data tracked in Analytics  

---

### 💡 Real Issues
❌ Personalization errors  
❌ Non-sendable DE used  
❌ Subscriber not in All Subscribers  
❌ Missing Send Classification  

---

### 💡 Key Takeaway
Email Studio is not just about sending emails  
👉 It is a combination of:  
**Data + Configuration + Personalization + Tracking**
