## 📩 Email Testing & Rendering Best Practices

---

### 🚨 The Problem
Ever sent a “perfect” email… but it looked broken on mobile or Outlook?  
👉 That’s where **Email Testing & Rendering** becomes critical in Salesforce Marketing Cloud.  

---

### 🔍 What is Email Rendering?
Email rendering = how your email appears across different devices, email clients, and screen sizes.  

👉 Same email can look:  
✅ Perfect in Gmail  
❌ Broken in Outlook  
⚠️ Misaligned on Mobile  

---

### ⚠️ Common Rendering Issues
❌ Images not loading  
❌ Broken layouts (especially in Outlook)  
❌ Font/style inconsistencies  
❌ Buttons not clickable  
❌ Dark mode issues  

---

### 🧪 Email Testing Checklist (Before Sending)
✔️ **Check Across Email Clients** (Gmail, Outlook, Yahoo, Apple Mail)  
✔️ **Mobile Responsiveness** → Test on different screen sizes, use responsive design (media queries)  
✔️ **Validate Links & CTAs** → All buttons clickable, no broken URLs  
✔️ **Image Optimization** → Use ALT text, compress images, avoid image-only emails  
✔️ **Spam Check** → Avoid spam trigger words, maintain text-to-image ratio  
✔️ **Personalization Testing** → AMPscript / Dynamic Content working correctly, no blank values  

---

### 🛠️ Tools You Should Use
🔧 Built-in **Preview & Test** in Salesforce Marketing Cloud  
🔧 **Litmus / Email on Acid** (advanced testing tools)  
🔧 Send **Test Emails** to real inboxes  

---

### 💡 Pro Tips (Real Project Level)
🔥 Always test in **Outlook** (most problematic client)  
🔥 Use **table-based HTML layout** (not div-heavy design)  
🔥 Keep email width ~600px  
🔥 Use **inline CSS** (external CSS often ignored)  
🔥 Test with **real data**, not dummy values  

---

### 🎯 Why This Matters?
📉 Bad rendering = Poor user experience  
📉 Broken emails = Lost conversions  

✅ Good rendering = Better engagement & trust  

---

### 💡 Key Takeaway
Email Testing & Rendering = **Quality Assurance for Campaigns**  
👉 A well-rendered email builds trust, improves engagement, and drives conversions.
