## 🚫 Why Your Emails Go to Spam

Sending emails but not getting opens?  
👉 Your emails might be landing in **SPAM** — and here’s why 👇  

---

### ⚠️ Top Reasons Emails Go to Spam

🔴 **1. Poor Sender Reputation**  
If your IP or domain has low trust, mailbox providers block or filter you.  

🔴 **2. No Email Authentication**  
Missing SPF, DKIM, or DMARC = suspicious sender.  

🔴 **3. Spammy Content**  
Too many words like “FREE”, “BUY NOW”, “URGENT” trigger spam filters.  

🔴 **4. Low Engagement Rate**  
If users don’t open/click → ISPs assume your emails are unwanted.  

🔴 **5. No Permission-Based Sending**  
Sending to users who never opted in = direct spam flag 🚩  

🔴 **6. High Bounce Rate**  
Invalid emails damage your sender score.  

🔴 **7. Missing Unsubscribe Link**  
Not giving users an exit option = compliance issue.  

---

### ✅ How to Fix It (Best Practices)
🟢 Warm up your IP gradually  
🟢 Use double opt-in subscribers  
🟢 Clean your data regularly (remove inactive users)  
🟢 Add SPF, DKIM & DMARC authentication  
🟢 Avoid spam trigger words  
🟢 Personalize your emails 🎯  
🟢 Maintain a healthy open & click rate  

---

### 💡 Pro Tip (SFMC)
Use **Data Views** like `_Open`, `_Click`, `_Bounce`  
to monitor engagement & clean inactive users regularly.  

---

### 🎯 Quick Check Before Sending
✔ Is my audience verified?  
✔ Is my content relevant?  
✔ Is my domain authenticated?  
✔ Am I sending to engaged users?  

---

### 📌 Final Thought
Inbox is **earned, not guaranteed**.  
👉 Focus on **trust + value + consistency** to stay out of spam.
